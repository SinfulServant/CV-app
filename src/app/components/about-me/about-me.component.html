About me

Who am I

My name is Ivan, I am 26 years old, now I live in Kharkov, and was born in Dniprodzherzhinsk.

Most of my life I studied music, I am a professional saxophonist. I have higher education at the Kharkiv State Academy of Culture, played in many groups, both large and small.

I`m currently a dedicated Christian, a member of the Evangelical Presbyterian Church of Kharkov.

Why I became a programmer?

I always believed that I had a more technical mindset, so I gravitated to technical activities and looked at music more like mathematics.

For several years I thought about starting to study IT and in September 2022 I decided to seriously do it. So I quit my job and devoted myself to the study of frontend technologies. Now I have been doing programming for more than a year and I like it more and more every day.
